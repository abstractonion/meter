# Composition Craft

A **meter** discipline module — the spatial layer. Composition decides where the eye lands first; editorial grids and Bauhaus asymmetry echo forward into dashboard and marketing layout.

## Core Philosophy

### Hierarchy is a single story

Every screen answers: what matters most, what supports it, what is noise. If two elements compete for first read, neither wins.

### Whitespace is structure, not leftover

Generous margins signal confidence. Crammed layouts signal fear of empty space. Negative space groups related items better than borders do.

### Cardlessness when possible

Cards are a scaffold, not a religion. Sections separated by rhythm and alignment often scan faster than boxed everything.
